About

About Me

I am Umme Kawser. I was born and raised in the small city of Cumilla, Bangladesh, and my upbringing has significantly influenced my contemporary persona. I am specialist in Geography and Environment, with a particular passion for the Environment, Physical Geography and Remote Sensing. My strong set of skills in Environmental, Ecological, Physical Geography and Social research is the result of my practical expertise in geospatial science, substantial laboratory work, and field studies. I am currently pursuing my second master's degree in Ecology and Remote Sensing at Monash University. I am a passionate researcher who possesses a blend of curiosity, expertise, and a desire to exceed expectations.
